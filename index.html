ANONYMOUS UNANIMOUS

Anonymous Unanimous enables individuals in a community to seek help from their neighbors via SMS. They scan a QR code, visit this site, enter their details, and when they send an SMS, the server forwards it to nearby registered users.

OUR SERVICES

  • QR Code Registration: Easy sign-up via QR.
  • SMS Requests: Send SMS even without internet.
  • No App Needed: Works via browser.
  • Location Filtering: Only nearby users are alerted.
  • Profanity Filter: Messages are scanned before being sent.
